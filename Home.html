Gene Quest: A Fun Game to Explore Genetics!
"Gene Quest" is an interactive and educational game that introduces kids to the fascinating world of genetics. In this game, players become young scientists who explore DNA, genes, and traits by solving puzzles and completing fun challenges. Each level teaches kids how genes are passed down from parents to offspring and how they affect physical characteristics like eye color, hair type, and more.
